Career Opportunities

University of the East - Manila

Software developers retrieve, store, and manipulate data to analyze a system's capability and requirements. They maintain the design and maintenance of a software system. At times they also oversee the work of computer technologists, programmers, and technicians.

Computer systems inside an enterprise are organized, installed, and supported by system administrators. These consist of intranets or internet caf√©s within their firm, as well as local area networks (LAN), wide area networks (WAN), and other data transmission systems.

To satisfy the business requirements of businesses and expand as those organizations develop, systems analysts implement, operate, and support IT and information systems. They produce specifications and requirements for programmers and developers to follow, evaluate data, write tests, and analyze data.

A firm hires a technical support person to manage and maintain their computer hardware and software systems. Their abilities are a benefit to the business since they aid in addressing technical problems involving client accounts or the software architecture of the corporation. By identifying and resolving typical issues, technical help will assist computer software integration as well. Employees with this designation typically start off in entry-level IT positions.

To maintain network security, network engineers are largely responsible for setting up, maintaining, and supporting firewalls. They are also in charge of purchasing tools like routers or switches that ensure the IT infrastructure of an organization operates as efficiently as possible.

Robotics engineers spend the majority of their time designing the plans and processes needed to not only build robots, but to have them work effectively. Some robotics engineers also design the machines that actually assemble the robots. Prior to a robot being constructed, engineers determine exactly what the robot will be used for. Since robots are meant to solve problems or alleviate human tasks, robotics engineers undergo an in-depth analysis of the job that the robot will be given.
